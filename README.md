# Bolt Food Croatia: Anatomy of an Exit

Interactive dashboard analyzing why Bolt Food exited Croatia in 2024 — with verified financial data from the Croatian Company Registry.

**[Live Dashboard](https://bolt-croatia-dashboard.vercel.app)**

## What's Inside

- **Revenue comparison**: Bolt Food vs Glovo vs Wolt (2022–2024) using CompanyWall.hr filings
- **Bolt ecosystem breakdown**: Food (exited) vs Rides (growing) vs Scooters (active)
- **Exit timeline**: Market entry to withdrawal (2018–2025)
- **Failure factors**: Data-backed analysis of why Bolt Food couldn't compete

## Key Findings

- Bolt Food Croatia generated just **€2.1M revenue** in its peak year — while Glovo hit **€18.7M** and Wolt **€12.4M**
- Bolt kept its **Rides** and **Scooters** businesses alive in Croatia — Food was the only exit
- The Croatian food delivery market is a duopoly: Glovo + Wolt control **~90%** of order volume

## Data Sources
